Learn Cebuano Through Cebuano!

A Brief Introduction

The only way to truly become fluent in a language is to expose yourself to material created in the target language. Why not try to do that as soon as you start?

Learn and practice Cebuano simply, by reading Cebuano. Here you will find articles and stories that are simple, yet will slowly introduce new grammar and vocabulary to you so that you can build upon your knowledge in the language.
